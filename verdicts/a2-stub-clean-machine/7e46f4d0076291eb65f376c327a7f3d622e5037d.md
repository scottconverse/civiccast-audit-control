`_classify_absence_via_detect` at
`civiccast/native/win_probes.py:593` returns `negative` only when
`detect_wsl_install(...) is False`. `True` and `None` remain `unreadable`.

The committed controls at `tests/native/test_win_probes.py:553` and
`tests/native/test_win_probes.py:567` passed. The auditor's additional
exit-0/unrecognized-stdout matrix returned:

- registered inventory: `unreadable`
- denied inventory plus SCM present: `unreadable`
- denied inventory plus SCM unknown: `unreadable`

A deliberate mutation from `installed is False` to `installed is not True`
caused the unknown-SCM control to fail with the unsafe result
`negative != unreadable`. The check therefore detects the exact fail-open
regression family it claims to detect.
