## Round-1 finding closure

### CC-EC-001 - Closed - HEVC with caption embedding is refused

**Files:** `civiccast/egress/gst/strategy.py:571-601`,
`tests/egress/test_gst_strategy.py:658-674`

`GstPlayoutStrategy._resolve_encoder_override()` is shared by both `start()` and
`reload_content()`. On Windows, an HEVC encoder override plus an active caption
embed request raises `EncoderUnavailableError` before graph construction. The
error explains that the inserter is H.264-only and gives both supported
remediations. Caption-free HEVC still reaches graph assembly.

The focused positive test passed. Restoring the round-1 `strategy.py` in a
disposable exact-SHA worktree made
`test_preflight_hevc_with_captions_refused` fail with `DID NOT RAISE`.

### CC-EC-002 - Closed - fallback helper copy is codec-accurate

**File:** `civiccast/apps/portal-operator/src/screens/ChannelOpsScreen.tsx:791-796`

The helper now limits the checkbox's CPU-fallback promise to H.264 and states
that the shipped HEVC/H.265 path requires hardware. This matches
`decide_encoder()`: absent hardware HEVC refuses even when fallback is enabled;
H.264 may select `openh264enc`.
